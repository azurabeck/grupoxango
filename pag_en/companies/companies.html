Companies in the Group

PSafe

Founded in 2011, PSafe is leader in digital security in Brazil. The company develops and offers free applications to increase privacy, protection and performance in mobile devices and PCs in Brazil and Latin America. Its main product is PSafe Total, Brazil's most downloaded apps on Google Play, with over 60 million downloads.

passeidireto.com

Passei Direto was created to revolutionize the experience of university students in Brazil and, in the future, the rest of Latin America. The site offers customized tools for each stage of a student’s academic period, providing assistance from the first day of class until graduation. The company’s mission is to optimize academic results, so that more and more students can say: “I passed.”
